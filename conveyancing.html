Welcome to JJJ, your trusted partner in conveyancing and property law in Victoria, Australia. We specialise in providing comprehensive legal services for all your property needs, ensuring a smooth and efficient transaction process.

Our Services Include:

  • Property Transfers: We handle all aspects of property transfers, including the preparation and lodgment of transfer documents, ensuring compliance with the Transfer of Land Act 1958 (Vic).
  • Land Subdivision: Our team assists with the creation and registration of Lodged Plans (LP) and Registered Plans (RP), guiding you through the subdivision process as per the Subdivision Act 1988 (Vic).
  • Stamp Duty and Tax Advice: We provide expert advice on stamp duty obligations and potential exemptions, liaising with the State Revenue Office Victoria to ensure accurate assessments.
  • Dispute Resolution: Our experienced team is equipped to handle disputes related to co-owned properties to achieve fair outcomes.
  • Verification of Identity: We ensure all parties involved in a transaction have their identities verified, maintaining the integrity and security of the conveyancing process.

At JJJ, we are committed to delivering personalised service and expert legal advice, making your property transactions as seamless as possible. Contact us today to learn how we can assist you with your conveyancing needs.
